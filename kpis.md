Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) are quantifiable metrics used to evaluate the success of an organization, employee, or project in meeting objectives. They provide evidence-based insights for decision-making, tracking progress on strategic goals like financial performance, marketing ROI (Return of investment), or operational efficiency.

Key characteristics of effective KPIs (SMART)

* Specific: Clearly defined to focus on a particular area
* Measurable: Quantitative (numbers, percentages) or qualitative data that can be tracked over time
* Achievable: Realistic goals for the team
* Relevant: Directly linked to business goals and strategic decisions
* Time-bound: Tracked over specific periods to measure progress
