A worked example is a step-by-step demonstration of how to solve a problem or complete a task, with all underlying thinking and processes explicitly laid out. By eliminating trail-and-error, they allow learners to focus purely on understanding the method rather than struggling to find the solution.

### 3 Steps of a Worked Example

1. Present: State the problem clearly
2. Model: Walk through the step-by-step procedure while verbalizing the thought process.
3. Arrive at the final answer to show what success looks like
